The Practice Soldering Kit is available at our shop as a standalone kit, and also comes free with any Beginner Pedal Kit.

This book will help you get equipped, teach you how to solder, walk you through assembling the practice kit, and ultimately prepare you to build DIY guitar pedals or any other hobby electronics.

The soldering practice kit contains a circuit board with a practice area and spare resistors you can use to learn and practice soldering. When you're starting to feel confident, you can solder buttons, LEDs, current limiting resistors to protect the LEDs, and a coin battery holder to turn it in to a keychain flashlight.

If you'd like to build your own DIY guitar pedal (or any other electronics), this kit will take you through the critical first steps.

[% WRAPPER "callout.html" type="tip" heading="Practicing Without the Kit" %]

Don't want to buy the practice kit? No problem!

You can use vero board, strip board, or proto board available from any electronics parts distributer. These are prototyping circuit boards with a grid of copper pads or strips. Also pick up some resistors, wire, or other components to practice soldering to the board.
