Your Beginner Pedal Kit comes with a soldering practice kit, a pre-drilled aluminum enclosure (optionally decorated with powder coating and UV printing, or a face plate), and the following parts:

Wrench

The kit includes a unique 3-in-1 wrench tool so you can tighten the nuts on the potentiometers, foot switch, and phone jacks.

Presorted Components

The components that might otherwise be difficult to identify are presorted in a labeled, cardboard tray.

Most kits throw all the parts in the bag, requiring you to read the codes or colored stripes printed on them. This can be a source of frustration and mistakes, so let's avoid that! If you get any mixed up, though, you can use this table to help identify them if needed.
