Look for writing on the shaft side of the potentiometer to indicate its value. For this pedal, the values are:

  • Volume: B100k or A100k
  • Gain: B10k

Insert the potentiometers into the enclosure. Refer to this image to make sure they are in the correct holes!

Secure the potentiometer on the outside by installing a washer followed by a nut. Use the provided red wrench tool to tighten the nut.

Check that both potentiometers are straight and level, as shown in the picture to the left. If not, loosen the nut and hold them in place while tightening again.
