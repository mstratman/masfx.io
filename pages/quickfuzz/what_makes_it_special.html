It's carefully designed to lower the barriers to DIY pedal building.

We want everyone with a passing interest in the hobby to give it a try, have fun, with low stakes, and avoid the common frustrations and pitfalls.

  • Low Cost: Without sacrificing the important areas of build quality
  • Free Soldering Practice Kit and "How To Solder" book: The #1 cause of failed builds is bad solder joints. Don't sacrifice a perfectly good pedal kit while trying to learn to solder.
  • Limited Tooling required: Just 2 tools! The included wrench and many little considerations (push-on knobs, no LED bezel, etc) keep things simple
  • Presorted Components: No need for you to read tiny codes or parse resistor color bands
  • No Off-Board Wiring: The most common point of errors and frustration is removed. All jacks and switches are on PCBs, with convenient ribbon cables connecting them.
  • Guts Easily Removed: Build first, decorate later. Easily pull it apart to fix problems
  • Variety of Documentation and Support: We do everything we can to ensure you have a fantastic experience.
