Bypass is fine, LED works, but no sound when engaged

If you can hear your clean tone when the pedal is bypassed, but you hit the foot switch, the LED comes on but no sound gets through, your input and output cables may be reversed.

Ensure your INPUT is on the right, OUTPUT is on the left.
